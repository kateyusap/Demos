**[Screen: off / speaker on camera]**

---

## PART 1 — PERSONA & PROBLEM
**[Screen: off / speaker on camera]**

> "Meet Sam — a supplier operations manager receiving dozens of purchase orders every week. The job sounds simple: confirm each order or flag what needs attention.
>
> But without AI, Sam's workbench is a flat unstructured list. Auto-confirm rules are outdated. Exceptions are handled one by one.
>
> The result: delays, missed delivery windows, and too much time spent on decisions that shouldn't need a human."

---

## PART 2 — VALUE DRIVERS
**[Screen: off / speaker or slide]**

> "Joule changes this in three ways: it configures the workbench the way Sam wants to work, bulk-confirms everything that qualifies automatically, and guides Sam through the exceptions — with AI-suggested responses ready to approve.
>
> Let me show you."

---

## PART 3 — DEMO
**[Screen recording starts — OC Joule Demo open in Chrome]**

---

### Scene 1 — Open Joule
**[Workbench: KPI tile "Items to Confirm — 14"]**

> "Sam has 14 open order lines. Sam opens Joule — it loads instantly and surfaces the first relevant action."

*[Click Joule sparkle icon — panel slides in]*

---

### Scene 2 — Reorganise the Workbench
**[Joule welcome screen]**

*[Click input — auto-types: "I want to configure how my pending orders are grouped"]*
*[Click Send]*

> "Joule reads the current grouping — Purchase Order, no sub-group — and offers to restructure it."

*[Click Option 1 — Yes]*

*[Click input — auto-types: "Group by Customer within each weekly delivery date"]*
*[Click Send]*

> "Joule shows a Before/After preview and asks for approval."

*[Click Option 1 — Yes]*

> "The table rebuilds instantly — grouped by delivery week, then customer. Sam can now see exactly where to focus."

---

### Scene 3 — Update Auto-Confirm Rules
*[Click input — auto-types: "Increase the value cap to $25,000 and reduce the buyer score to 80"]*
*[Click Send]*

> "Joule surfaces all five current rules, proposes the two changes, and waits for Sam's sign-off."

*[Click Option 1 — Yes]*

> "Rules updated."

---

### Scene 4 — Bulk Confirm 7 Items
**[Joule spinner: "Analysing your pending purchase orders…"]*

> "Joule analyses all 14 items against the new rules. The result: 7 ready to auto-confirm, 3 need review, 2 escalated by buyers."

*[Click Option 1 — Yes, confirm all 7]*
*[Click Option 1 — Yes, confirm now]*

> "Wave 1, Wave 2 — done. 7 confirmations generated. The items disappear from the table. Sam didn't touch a single one."

---

### Scene 5 — Resolve Exceptions & Escalations
*[Click Option 1 — Review 3 items that need input]*

> "For the 3 exceptions, Joule reads the context and suggests the right fix for each — adjusted quantities, revised dates, price notes. Sam accepts all three."

*[Click Option 1 — Accept all 3 and confirm]*

*[Click Option 1 — Review escalated items]*

> "The 2 escalated items are high cancel-risk. Joule surfaces the conflict — buyer needs April 20, Sam can ship April 25 — and offers three resolution paths. Sam picks one."

> "Final summary: all 12 items processed. 11 confirmed, 1 escalated to the account manager."

## Recording Notes

| Tip | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Pace** | Pause 1–2 sec after each Joule response before speaking |
| **Cursor** | Move slowly, hover briefly before each click |
| **Banner** | Dismiss the yellow Test Build banner with ✕ before recording |
| **Reset** | Cmd+R to reload between takes |
| **Resolution** | 1920×1080 recommended |
| **Cuts** | Natural cut points: after Scene 1, after Scene 4, after Scene 5 |
